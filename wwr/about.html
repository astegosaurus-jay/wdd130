History

Our story started with a simple dream, to white water raft the great rivers of America and protect our knees while doing it. Now our goal is to take people on amazing Adventure and show them the beuaty of the great outdoors. We started with one raft and have grown to span over 8 states and plans for expainsons continue. Now the question that you need to ask yourself is are you ready to take on some of the greatest challenges the outdoors has to offer? and dont forget your knee pads!
